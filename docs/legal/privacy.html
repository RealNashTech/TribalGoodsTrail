Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Tribal Goods Trail is operated by Real Nash Tech & Consulting (Sole Proprietorship), Woodburn, Oregon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Business submission details (name, description, category, location, contact).
  • User-provided contact emails for communication.
  • Approximate device location for nearby business results.
  • Diagnostics such as analytics, device info, and crash logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display and verify Native-owned businesses.
  • To communicate with business owners about their listings.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.

Location Information

  • Used to calculate distance to nearby businesses.
  • Not stored as a location history.

Business Submissions

Information submitted may be displayed publicly. Only submit information you are authorized to share.

Data Sharing

  • No sale of personal data.
  • Limited sharing with trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., Firebase).

Data Security

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Restricted administrative access.

Business Definitions & Vendor Responsibility

Listings include both official Native-owned businesses and community sellers. Sellers are responsible for compliance with business, tax, safety, and cultural laws.

No Verification of Business Status

We do not verify Tribal enrollment, business licenses, food safety permits, or cultural authenticity.
