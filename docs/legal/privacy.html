Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Tribal Goods Trail is operated by Real Nash Tech & Consulting (Sole Proprietorship), Woodburn, Oregon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business submission details (business name, description, location, contact, category).
  • User-provided contact emails for communication about listings.
  • Approximate device location for “nearby businesses” features.
  • Diagnostics: analytics, crash logs, and device diagnostics from platform providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display Native-owned businesses and related details in the app.
  • To verify submissions and communicate about listing status.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. Location Information

  • Used to calculate distance to nearby businesses.
  • Not stored as a location history; not used to track movements.

4. Business Submissions

  • Information you submit may be displayed publicly after review.
  • You should only submit information you are authorized to share.

5. Data Sharing

  • No sale of personal data.
  • Limited sharing with trusted services (e.g., Firebase) solely to operate the app.
  • No third-party marketing trackers receive personal information.

6. Data Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest through our infrastructure providers.
  • Administrative access is restricted to the verified operator.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request corrections or removal of your submission data where feasible.
  • You may disable location permissions; nearby results may be limited.
