Content Moderation Policy

We may edit, pause, or remove content or listings that violate these standards:

  • False, misleading, or unverified claims about Native ownership or affiliation.
  • Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or threats.
  • Fraudulent offers, scams, or deceptive promotions.
  • Content that violates laws or infringes rights.

No Verification of Business Status

We do not verify Tribal enrollment, business registration, licenses, or food safety compliance. Sellers self-identify and are responsible for truthful representation.
